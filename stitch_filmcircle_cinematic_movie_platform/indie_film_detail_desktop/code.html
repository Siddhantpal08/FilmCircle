Elena Vance

Director • Writer • Visual Artist

Elena Vance is a visionary filmmaker known for her minimalist approach to storytelling. Her work often explores themes of urban isolation and the intersection of technology and human emotion. "The Silent Rhythm" marks her third feature film, cementing her place as a leading voice in modern indie cinema.
